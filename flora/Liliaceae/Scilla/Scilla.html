Genus: Scilla

Genus description:

Bulbous perennials.Bulb perennial, ovoid to globose, composed of numerous free scales, progessively renewed each year.Flowers solitary or in a raceme or corymb.Bracts 0 to 1 subtending each flower; bracteoles absent.Perianth segments free, usually blue or purple, rarely white.Filamentsfree, usually slender, inserted at base of periant.Capsule subglobose, 3-lobed.Seeds 1-10 in each loculus, globose to ellipsoid, unwingedMany species, including several from S.W.Asia are cultivated for ornament.
