Species: Convolvulus altheoides 

Family: Convolvulaceae  
Genus:  Convolvulus
Species description: Pubescent perennial. Stems up to 100 cm, slender, trailing or twining, herbaceous. Leaves petiolate, very variable but at least the upper deeply lobed and cordate to sagitate. Peduncles axillary, usually much longer than bracts, 1- to 3-flowered. Sepals variable, acute or rounded. Corolla 25-40 mm, usually pink

Field Notes:

Habitat: Dry places

Distribution: S.Europe Al Bl Bu Co Cr Ga Gr Hs It Ju Lu Rm Sa Si Tu
