Species: Cistus ladanifer 

Family: Cistaceae  
Genus:  Cistus
Species description: Stems up to 250 cm, erect, very viscid, Leaves 40-80(120) x 6-25 mm, linear-lanceolate, 3-veined for 1/3 their length, green and glabrous above, densely white-tomentose beneath, subsessile. Sepals 3. Flowers 7-10 cm in diameter, solitary, white, with or without a crimson spot at the base of each petal. Style very short. Ovary 10-locular
