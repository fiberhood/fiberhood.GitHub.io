Species: Cistus crispus 

Family: Cistaceae  
Genus:  Cistus
Species description: Rounded bush up to 50 cm Leaves 10-40 x 4-15 mm. oblong to elliptical, undulate, 3-veined, greyish-green, villous-tomentose, with mixed stellate and long, simple hairs, sessile. Cymes few-flowered, dense. Pedicels 1-5 mm, densely villous. Flowers 3-4 cm in diameter, purplish-red. Style filiform.
