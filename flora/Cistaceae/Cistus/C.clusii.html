Species: Cistus clusii 

Family: Cistaceae  
Genus:  Cistus
Species description: Stems up to 100 cm, erec, much branched.Leaves 10-25 x 1-2 mm, linear, dark green above, white-tomentose beneath, with revolute margins, subsessile.Inflorescence up to 12 flowered with a terminal, umbel-like cyme and often with opposite pairs of flowers or 3-flowerde cymes below.Peduncles, pedicels and calyx clothed with long, white hairs.Sepals 3, 5-8 mm.Flowers 2-3 cm in diameter, white.Style short

Field Notes:

Habitat:

Distribution: W. & C.Mediterranean region, from S.Spain to S.E Italy. Bl Hs It. Si

Found: S.Spain: Rio Segura
