Species: Aquilegia atrata 

Family: Ranunculaceae  
Genus:  Aquilegia
Species description: Stems 40-80 cm, densely pilose, at least above Basal leaves 2-ternate, leaflets 2- to 3-fid, more or less glabrous.Flowers nodding, dark purple-violet. Perianth-segments 15-24 x 8-9 mm; limb of honey-leaf 8-12 x 7-9 mm; spur 10-15 x 4 mm, hooked.Stamen long-exerted.Follicles glandular-pubescent.
