Genus: Putoria

Genus description:

Dwarf shrubs. Leaves opposite; stipules small, interpetiolar, more or less fused. Flowes in small fascicles. Calyx 4-toothed, persistent in fruit. Corolla infundibuliform with a long tube, 4-lobed. Style filiform, with short 2-lobed stigma. Ovary 2-locular with ine ovule in each loculus. Fruit a drupe with 2 pyrenes
