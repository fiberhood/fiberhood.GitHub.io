Species: Putoria calabria 

Family: Rubiaceae  
Genus:  Putoria
Species description: Much branched, forming mats up to 1 m in diameter, foetid, glabrous to densely puberulent. Leaves 10-20 x c. 3mm, obovate to elleptic-lanceolate, obtuse wuth revolute margin; stipules c. 1 mm, ovate. Corolla 10-15 mm, pink, the lobes 3-4 mm, linear-lanceolate. Fruit c. 5 mm, black.
