Genus: Moricandia

Genus description:

Glabrous annual or perennial herbs. Leaves simple, fleshy. Inflorescence corymbose. Inner sepals saccate at base; petals purple, rarely whitsh. Fruit a siliqua; Valves with a distinct median vein; style short, stigma 2-lobed. Seeds in 1 or 2 rows in each loculus.
