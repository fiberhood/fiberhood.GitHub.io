Species: Cardamine hirsuta 

Family: Brassicaceae  
Genus:  Cardamine
Species description: Annual 7-30 cm; Stems glabrous. Lowest leaves in a distinct rosette, pinnate, with 1-3(-5) pairs of obovate to orbicular lateral leaflets and a larger, reniform terminal leaflet; Cauline leafs2-4, smaller than the basal leaves, with 2-5 pairs of smaller leaflets; leaflets angular, hairy on the upper surface. Pedicels 1-2 mm at anthesis. Petals 2.5-3 mm or absent, not exceeding the sepals; stamens 4(-6). Sliqua 18-25 x c. 1 mm, erect, overtopping flowers; style 0.5-1 mm, conical. Seeds 0.9-1 x 0.7-0.8 mm
