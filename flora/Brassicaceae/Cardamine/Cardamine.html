Genus: Cardamine

Genus description:

Annual to perennial herbs; glabrous or with unbrached hairs. Leaves simple to pinnate. Inner sepals slightly sccate; petals white or purple(rarely pale yellow). Fruit a strongly compressed siliqua; valves coiling spirally from the base at dehiscence, veinless or with distinct median vein; style short or distinc; stigma slightly 2-lobed. Seeds in 1 row in each loculus.
