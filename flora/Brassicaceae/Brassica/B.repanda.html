Species: Brassica repanda 

Family: Brassicaceae  
Genus:  Brassica
Species description: Laxly to densely caespitose; Stems up to 50 cm, usually leafless, glabrous or hairy. Basal leaves 1-15 cm entire to pinnatipartite. Racemes 2- to 35-flowered. Petals 7-30 mm, yellow. Siliqua 10-80 x 1-4.5 mm, variable in shape and proporions, gradually or abruptly attenuate into a seedless beak.

Field Notes:

Habitat: Usually in rocky and stony places.

Distribution: S.W.Europe. Ga Hs It

Found: S.Spain: road to Grazalema

Date: 23-May-93

Flora Europea:1-338
