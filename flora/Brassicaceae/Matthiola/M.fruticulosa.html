Species: Matthiola fruticulosa 

Family: Brassicaceae  
Genus:  Matthiola
Species description: Sparsely pubescent to densely white-tomentose perennial, up to 60 cm, woody at base. Leaves linear to oblong, entire to sinuate-pinnatifid. Pedicels 0-3(-8) mm in fruit. Sepals 6-14 mm. Petals 12-28 mm, yellow to purle-red. Siliqua 25-120 x 1-2(-3) mm, more or less cylindrical; stigma without hornsor with horns up to 3 mm long.
