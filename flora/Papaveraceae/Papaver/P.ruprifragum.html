Species: Papaver ruprifragum 

Family: Papaveraceae  
Genus:  Papaver
Species description: Ascending, many-stemmed, subglabrous perrenial up to 50 cm. Leaves pinnatisect, sparcely hispid on midrib and petiole, mostly basal, with persistent bases; segments irregular, oblong to lanceolate, aristate. Pedicel sparcely hispid or subglabrous. Petals 20-25 mm, brick-red. Filaments subulate. Capsule c. 2.5 mm, oblong-clavate, faintly ribbed. Stigmatic disc pyramidal; rays 8

Field Notes:

Habitat: Crevices in mountain rocks

Distribution: S.Spain (S de Ronda, near Grazalama). Hs

Found: S.Spain: Serrania de Ronda, ± North side of the Puèrto de las Palomas (Grazalema)

Date: 8-6-93; 6-5-94

Flora Europea:1-249

Literatuur:

Notes: Identification with Fl.Europea: correct, except colour: not brick-red but orange !!
