Species: Campanula spicata 

Family: Campanulaceae  
Genus:  Campanula
Species description: Hairy biennial. Stem up to 70 cm or more, erect, striate. Basal leaves entire, linaer-lanceolate; cauline linear, acuminate. Inflorescence long, interrupted at the base, many-flowered. Calyx-teeth ovate-accuminate, c. 1/3 as long as the infundibuliform corolla. Corolla 17-20 mm.

Field Notes:

Habitat: Mountain meadows

Distribution: Alps, extending southwards to C.Italy and Crna Gora(Ju)

Found: C.Jugoslavia: Bosnie-Herzegovina

Date: 8-7-76
