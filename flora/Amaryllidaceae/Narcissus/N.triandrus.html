Species: Narcissus triandrus 

Family: Amaryllidaceae  
Genus:  Narcissus
Species description: Flowering in spring;Bulb 12-20 x 8-17 mm.Leaves 15-30 x 1.5-3 mm, dark green.Scape about equalling leaves.Flowers drooping, white to bright yellow.Spathe 15-40 mm.Pedicels 8-40 mm.Hypanthial tube 10-20 mm.Perianth segments 10-20(-30) mm, lanceolate to linear-oblong, sharply deflected; Corona 5-15(-25) mm x 7-25 mmVariable, much cultivated

Field Notes:

Habitat: Hedges, woods, and rocky pastures

Distribution: Spain and Portugal; N.W.France Ga Hs Lu

Found: S.Spain: wood along road from Rio.Madera => Segura de la Sierra

Date: 25-4-94

Flora Europea:5-82
