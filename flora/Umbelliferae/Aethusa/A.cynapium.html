Species: Aethusa cynapium 

Family: Umbelliferae  
Genus:  Aethusa
Species description: Annual or sometimes biennial 5-100 cm. Leaves deltate in outline; lobes lanceolate or ovate, pinnatifid. Rays (4-)10-20. Bracts usually absent; bracteoles usually 3-4, deflexed, subulate, on outer side of the partial umbels. Fruit 3-4 cm.

Field Notes:

Habitat: A weed of cultivated land. Rarely in woods.

Distribution: Most of Europe, but rare in the Mediterranean region. Au Be Br Bu Co Cz Da Fe Ga Ge He Ho Hs Hu It Ju No Po Rm Rs(N, B, C, W, E) Su

Found: In the north of the Netherlands
