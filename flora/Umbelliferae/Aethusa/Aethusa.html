Genus: Aethusa

Genus description:

Leaves 2(-3)-pinnate. Sepals absent. Petals white, obcordate, the outer larger; apex inflexed. Fruit ovoid, somewhat compressed dorsally. Ridges thick, the lateral narrowly winged; vittea solitary.
