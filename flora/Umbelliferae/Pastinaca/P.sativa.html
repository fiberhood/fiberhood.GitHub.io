Species: Pastinaca sativa 

Family: Umbelliferae  
Genus:  Pastinaca
Species description: More or less pubescent biennial up to 100 cm. Stem hollow or solid, angled or terete. Basal leaves usually simple pinnate, rarely (subsp sativa var fleischmannii) 2-pinnatisect; secondary veins inconspicious; segments (2-)5-11, acute or obtuse, crenate-dentate, the teeth with a cartilaginous mucro; petioles slender, not spongy. Rays 5-20, more or less angled. Bracts and bracteoles 0-2 mm, caducous. Fruit 5-7 mm, broadly elliptical; wing 0.25 - 0.5 mm wide; vittae on the commissural face not reaching the ends of the fruit.
