Genus: Pastinaca

Genus description:

Leaves simple or pinnate; segments sometimes deeply lobed. Sepals absent. Petals yellow, ovate, incurved. Fruit elliptical, strongly compressed dorsaly; lateral ridges winged, the vascular bundels near the outer edge of the wing; vittae 1, rarely 2.
