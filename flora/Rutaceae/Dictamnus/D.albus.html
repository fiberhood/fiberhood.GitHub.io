Species: Dictamnus albus 

Family: Rutaceae  
Genus:  Dictamnus
Species description: Bushy, densely leafy perennial 40-80 cm, more or less woody below; stems and branches glandular-punctate, thinly hairy. Leaflets 3-6 pairs, lanceolate to ovate, pubescent at least on the principal veins beneath; inflorescence-axis, pedicels, bracts and sepals desely furnished with short, patent hairs and dark, stipitate glands. Sepals lanceolate. Petals 2-2.5 cm, elliptic-lanceolate, white to pink or bluish, with purple dots or streaks. Filaments about as long as the petals, glandular above.
