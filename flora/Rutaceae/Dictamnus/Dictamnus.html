Genus: Dictamnus

Genus description:

Perennial herbs with alternate, pellucide-punctuate, imparipinnate leaves. Flowers large and showy, in terminal, bracteate racemes. Sepals 5. Petals 5, narrow, the lowest declinate. Stamens 10; filaments declinate and upwardly curving. Styles connate. Capsule hard, deeply 5-lobed.
