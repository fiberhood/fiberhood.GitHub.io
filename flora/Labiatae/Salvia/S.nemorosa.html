Species: Salvia nemorosa 

Family: Labiatae  
Genus:  Salvia
Species description: Stems 30-60 cm, many, more or less appressed- or patent-pubescent, eglandular. Basal leaves simple, petiolate, oblong, cordate or rounded at base, attenuate at apex, regularly crenate, mor or less pubescent; cauline leaves more or less sessile. Inflorescence usually dense; verticilasters with 2-6 flowers. Bracts as long as or longer than calyx, ovate , rounded at base, violet, imbricate in but; pedicels c. 2 mm. Calyx6-7 mm. Corolla 8-12(-14) mm, violet blue, rarely pink or white.
