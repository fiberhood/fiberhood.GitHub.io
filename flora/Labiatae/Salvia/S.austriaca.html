Species: Salvia austriaca 

Family: Labiatae  
Genus:  Salvia
Species description: Stems up to 100 cm, erect, usually simple, glandular-hairy. Basal leaves simple, petiolate, irregulary toothed, pubescent beneath, glabrous above; Cauline leaves few, sessile or absent. Verticilasters with 4-9 flowers. Calyx 8-10 mm, glandular pubescent. Corolla 12-17 mm, yellowisch - white. Stamens long exserted.

Field Notes:

Habitat:

Distribution: From S.Csechoslovakia and N.Ukraine southwards to N.E bulgaria. Au Bu Hu Ju Ru Rs (W, K, E)

Found: Hu: near Matra and Buck; Ru: N.E, stroomgebied vd Prut
