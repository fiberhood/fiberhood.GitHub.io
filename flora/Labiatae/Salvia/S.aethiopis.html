Species: Salvia aethiopis 

Family: Labiatae  
Genus:  Salvia
Species description: Biennal or perennial 30-100 cm.Stems robust, erect, white-lanate, eglandular, more or less leafless above.Leaves simple, petiolate, more or less ovate, acuminate, deeply and irregularly serrate, lanate when young.Verticillasters with 6-10 flowers.Bracts slightly shorter than calyx, green or violet-tinged.Calyx 5-10 mm, lanate.Corolla 10-15 mm, white, upper lip weakly falcate.
