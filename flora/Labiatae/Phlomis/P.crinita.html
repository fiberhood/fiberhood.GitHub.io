Species: Phlomis crinita 

Family: Labiatae  
Genus:  Phlomis
Species description: Shrub up to 70 cmStem eglandular.Lower leaves 4-11.5 cm, ovate to lanceolate, codate or rounded at base, entire, coriaceous, stellate-lanate on both sufaces, abruptly contracted into a distinct petiole up to 7 cm.Floral leaves sessile, ovate, gradualy tapering into an acute or acuminate apex.Verticillasters 2- to 10-flowered.Bracteoles 13-15 mm, linear, straight at apex, stellate-villous.Calyx 13-17 mm, stellate-villous.; teeth 2-5 mm, linear-anceolate, straight at apex.Corolla 20-25 mm, brownish-yellow or yellowNutlets blabrous
