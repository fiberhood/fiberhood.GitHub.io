Genus: Cotinus

Genus description:

Polygamous or dioecious shrubs or small trees, often with recinous bark. Leaves simlpe and entire. Buds with several imbricate scales. Flowers small, in axillary or terminal panicles. Petals 5. Stamens 5. Placentation basal. Pedicels in fruit long, slender with long, patent hairs. Styles lateral.
