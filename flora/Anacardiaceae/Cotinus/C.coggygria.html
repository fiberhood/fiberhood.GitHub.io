Species: Cotinus coggygria 

Family: Anacardiaceae  
Genus:  Cotinus
Species description: Rounded, glabrous shrub up to 5 m. Leaves 3-8 cm, ovate to obovate, glaucus; petioles not winged. Inflorecscence 15020 cm, terminal, with long. slender branches. Pedicels numerous, many without fruits, all plumose. Drupe 3-4 mm, reniform.

Field Notes:

Habitat: Dry, rocky slopes.

Distribution: S.Europe, from S.E.France eastwards, and extending northwards to S.E. Czechslovakia and C.Ukraine. Al Au Bu Cr Cz Ga Gr He Hu It Ju Rm Rs(W,K,E) Tu [Ge Hs]
