Species: Epilobium hirsutum 

Family: Onagraceae  
Genus:  Epilobium
Species description: Stolons hypogeal, fleshy. Stems up to 200 cm, robust, usually villous or tomentose. Leaves 2-12 x 0.5-3.5 cm, oblong to lanceolate, sessile, semi-amplexicaul, sharply serrulate. Petals (6-)10-16 mm, bright purplish pink, Seeds 1-1.5 mm, obovoid.
