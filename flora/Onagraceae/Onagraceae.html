Family: Onagraceae

Family description:

Herbs or shrubs. Flowers Hermaphrodite, actinomorphic or weakly zygomorphic. Hypanthial tube ("calyx tube") often prosent. Sepals 2, 4 or 5; petals 0, 2, 4 or 5. Stamens 2 or 4 in 1 whorl, or 8 or 10 in 2 whorls; polenconnected in masses by fine threads. Style 1; ovary inferior, 1-, 2-, 4-, or 5-locular. Fruit a capsule, a berry or dry and indehisent; seeds without endosperm.
