Species: Linaria triornithophora 

Family: Scrophulariaceae  
Genus:  Linaria
Species description: Glabrous, somewhat glaucus perennial; stems 50-130 cm, erect or diffuse, simple or branched above. Leaves 25-75 x 5-30 mm, lanceolate to ovate-lanceolate, acute, in remote whorles of 3(-5). Inflorescence up to 10 cm, lax, with 3-10 flowers, mostly in whorls; pedicels 10-30 mm, erect. Calyx 9-10 mm; lobes ovate-lanceolate, long acuminate, equal. Corolla 35-55 mm, purple with yellow palate; spur 16-25 mm. ...

Field Notes:

Habitat: Hedges and thickets

Distribution: N.W & W.C Soain, N. & C. Portugal. Hs Lu

Found: C.W.Spain: Sierra de Pe–a de Francia
