Species: Digitalis lutea ssp lutea 

Family: Scrophulariaceae  
Genus:  Digitalis
Species description: Glabrous or slightly oubescent perennial 60-100 cm. Leaves oblong-oblanceolate, serrate to subentire. Racemes more or less dense, many-flowered. Calyx-lobes linear-lanceolate, acute, glandular-ciliate. Corolla 9-25 mm, pale yellow to witish; lateral lobes of lower lip ovate; middle lobe 5-6 mm, ovate or elliptical
