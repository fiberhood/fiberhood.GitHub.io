Species: Digitalis obscura 

Family: Scrophulariaceae  
Genus:  Digitalis
Species description: Glabrous shrub, 30-120 cm; stems leafless below, densely leafy above, decumbent to erect. Leaves linear-oblong to lanceolate, entire to serrate, coriaceous, shiny. Racemes elongate, somewhat secund. Corolla 20-30 mm. orange-yellow to brown, reticulate or spotted. Capsule conical, acute, exceeding the calyx;

Field Notes:

Habitat: Bushy and rocky places

Distribution: E, C & S Spain; Hs

Found: S.Spain: Serrania de Ronda: Near Aldebaran; throughout S. & C.Spain

Flora Europea: 3-239
