Species: Digitalis purpurea ssp mariana 

Family: Scrophulariaceae  
Genus:  Digitalis
Species description: subsp mariana: Lower leaves broadly ovate, abruptly contracted at base, white tomentose. Pedicels longer than calyx. Corolla purle, glabrous outside

Field Notes: 1993 and 1994: only on one place in the Sierra Morena: In 1964: at different places in this sierra.

Habitat: Rocky slopes and crevices

Distribution: S.C.Spain, N.E.Portugal. Hs Lu

Found: S.C.Spain: Sierra Madrona in the E.Sierra Morena.

Date: 12-6-93; 28-4-94
