Species: Euphorbia amygdaloides ssp amigdaloides 

Family: Euphorbiaceae  
Genus:  Euphorbia
Species description: Pubescent, caespitose perennial,Stems up to 90 cm, with up to 30 axillary rays.Leaves (10-)25-71(-130) x (5-)10-20(-30) mm, oblanceolate to obovate or spathulate, entire.Ray-leaves broadly ovate.Raylet-leaves partially or wholly connate, rarely more or less free(var.ligulata)Rays (3-)5-11, up to 4 times dichotomous.Glands with 2 horns.Capsule 3-4 x 2.5-4 mm, deeply sulcate, punctuate.Seeds 2-2.5 mm, ovoid, blackish.ssp amigdaloides: flowering in 2th year. Leaves of the 1th years growth larger than those of the 2th , and crowded on top of the stem.
