Species: Mucizonia hispida 

Family: Crassulaceae  
Genus:  Mucizonia
Species description: Usually glandular-pubescent, at least in the florescence, but sometimes glabrous.Stem 8-15 cm, diffuse, branched from base.Leaves 12-18 mm, oblong, terete, steaked with red.Flowers long stalked, in lax cymes.Corolla 8-13 mm, yellowish-green tinged with pink; lobes rather shorter than tube.
