Genus: Nonea

Genus description:

Annual or perennial, hispid herbs. Flowers in bracteate, terminal cymes. Calyx lobed to 1/4 - 2/3, accrescent. Corolla yellow, purple, brown or white, with cykindical tube and infundibuliform limb, with 5 hairy or lacinate scales, or 5 tufts of hairs, or a ring of hairs in throat. Stamens included in corolla-tube; inserted at about or above the middle of the tube. Style included; stigma entire or bifid. Nutlets ovoid to reniform, erect or oblique, smoth or ribbed, with a thickened collar-like ring at the base.
