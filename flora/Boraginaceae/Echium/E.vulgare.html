Species: Echium vulgare 

Family: Boraginaceae  
Genus:  Echium
Species description: Erect, hispid biennial (or sometimes perrennial ?) 20-90 cm, with 1 to several flowering stems. Leaves with rather soft, appressed setae; the basal and lower cauline 50-150 x 10-20 mm, elliptical to lanceolate, attenuate and petiolate at base; the upper narrowly lanceolate, sessile. Inflorescence more or less spike-like to panniulate. Calyx 5-7 mm. Corolla 10-19 mm, blue to bluish-violet, with 4-5 long-excerted stamens.

Very variable and requiring further study.
