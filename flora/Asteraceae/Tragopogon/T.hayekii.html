Species: Tragopogon hayekii 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Tragopogon
Species description: Like T.pratensis, but stems branched; ligules golden yellow, exceeding the bracts; beak of achene 1.5-3 times as long as body

(T.Pratensis: Annual to perennial with a cylindrical rootstock. Stems 30-70 cm, often simple, glabrescent. Leaves linear-lanceolate, more or less subamplexicaule at base. Peduncles not inflated. Involucral bracts c. 8. Ligules yellow. Achenes 1-1.5 cm, more or less squamose.Most of Europe.)

Field Notes:

Habitat: Meadows
