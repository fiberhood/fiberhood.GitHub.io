Species: Pallensis spinosa ssp spinosa 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Pallensis
Species description: Up to 60 cm, softly hairy. Stems hard, woody at the base, usually branched, the braches overtopping the main stem. Leaves lanceolate to elliptical, obtuse, mucronate; basal petiolate; cauline sessile and semi-amplexicaule. Outer involuvral bracts 1.5-3.5 cm, ovate, coriaceous below, with a long, patent, spine-tipped leaflike apex exceeding the ligules; inner ovate, coriaceous, with or without a short, narrow, green apex. Ligules deeply 3-toothed at apex; tubular florets 5-lobed. Achenes 2-2.5 ..
