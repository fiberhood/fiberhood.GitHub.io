Genus: Pallensis

Genus description:

Annual to biennial herbs. Stems leafy. Capitula medium, terminating the branches. Involucral bracts in 2-3 rows. Receptacle convex, with scales. Ligulate florets in 2 rows, female, yellow; tubular florets numerous, the tube compressed and sometimes with with 1-2 narrow wings. Outer achenes flat, winged; inner slightly compressed, not or scarecely winged; pappus of numerous short hyaline scales.
