Species: Onopordum acanthium 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Onopordum
Species description: Plant up to 300cm. Stems yellowish , more or less hairy, the wings not reticulate-veined, with spines up to 5 mm. Laeves up to 35 x 20 cm, oblong-ovate to lanceolate or ovate, sessile, sinuate-dentate or with 6-8 pairs of broadly triangular teeth with an apical spine 5-10 mm. greyish-green and sparcely lanate above. Capitula subglobose, solitary or in teminal clusters of 2-5; involucral bracts gradually tapering into a spine up to 5 mm, not keeled, puberulent outside. Corolla 15-25 mm, purplish to white. Achenes 4-5 mm, marbled greyish-black; pappus 7/9 mm, pale redish.
