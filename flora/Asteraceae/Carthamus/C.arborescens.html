Species: Carthamus arborescens 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Carthamus
Species description: Much branched, densely glandular perennial up to 2.5 m, woody at the base. Upper cauline leaves ovate to lanceolate-ovate, pinnatifid, spinose-dentate. Capitula up to 40 mm in diameter, broadly ovoid. Inner involucral bracts oblon- to ovate-lanceolate, entire to denticulate towards the apex. Corolla yellow. Achenes oblond to obpyramidal, rugulose. Pappus-scales verry narrow, densely ciliate, decidous.
