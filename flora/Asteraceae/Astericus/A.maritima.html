Species: Astericus maritima 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Astericus
Species description: Scabrid, hispid, perrenial. Stems rarely more than 20 cm, woody, much brached, ascending. Leaves oblong to olong-spathulate, petiolate. Outer involucral bracts c. 1 cm, coraceous below, with an obtuse, spathulate apex, equalling or shorter than the ligules. Ligules deeply 3-toothed; tubular florets 5-lobed. Achenes c. 1.5 mm; papus 1-1.5 mm

Field Notes:

Habitat: Maritime rocks.

Distribution: W. part of theMediterranean region, S.Portugal; W. & S.Greece. Bl Co Ga Gr Hs It Lu Sa Si

Found: S.Spain: Cabo da Gato

Date: 26-May-93; 2-5-94
