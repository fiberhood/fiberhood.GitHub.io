Species: Cirsium acaule 

Family: Asteraceae  
Genus:  Cirsium
Species description: Perennial, acaulescent or rarely with stem 5-15(-35) cm. Leaves herbaceous, undulate, pblong to oblong-lanceolate, pinnatisect; segments ovate to suborbicular, with 2-5 spinose-detate lobes. Involucre 20-32 x (10-)16-25(-30)mm; bracts appressed, the vittae absent or indistinct. Corolla 23-35 mm, purple. Achenes 3-5 mm.
