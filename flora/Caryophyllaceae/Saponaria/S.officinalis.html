Species: Saponaria officinalis 

Family: Caryophyllaceae  
Genus:  Saponaria
Species description: Perennial. Stems 30-90 cm, erect, usually glabrous, simple or brached above. Leaves ovate to ovate-lanceolate, 3-veined, acute. Inflorscence condensed, with oppiste branches bearing few-flowerd dichasia. Flowers large shortly pedicellate, usually flesh-coloured. Calyx c.20 mm, glabrous or rarely hairy, green or reddish; teeth triangular, acute. Limp of petal c. 10 mm, moreor less entire.
