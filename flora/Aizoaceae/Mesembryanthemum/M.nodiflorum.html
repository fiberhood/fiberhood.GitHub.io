Species: Mesembryanthemum nodiflorum 

Family: Aizoaceae  
Genus:  Mesembryanthemum
Species description: More or less procumbent annual up to 20 cm. Leaves narrowly oblong, subterete, obtuse, glaucus and slightly cristalline-papilose. Flowers terminaland axillary, solitary, subsessile. Petals shorter than sepals, Yellowish or white.

Field Notes:

Habitat: Maritime sands and salt-marshes.

Distribution: Mediterranium region extending to C.Portugal. Bl Co Cr Ga Gr Hs It Ju Lu Sa Si [Az]

Found: S.Spain, Costa del Sol

Date: 27-May-93

Flora Europea:1-113
