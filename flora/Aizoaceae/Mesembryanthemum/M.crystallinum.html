Species: Mesembryanthemum crystallinum 

Family: Aizoaceae  
Genus:  Mesembryanthemum
Species description: Like C.nodiflorum but whole plant densely crystalline-papillose; leaves spathulate to broadly ovate, flat; petals longer than sepals

Field Notes:

Habitat: Maritime sands and salt-marshes.

Distribution: Mediterranium region extending to C.Portugal. Bl Co Cr Ga Gr Hs It Ju Lu Sa Si [Az]

Found: S.Spain, Costa del Sol; 1994:Cabo da Gata

Date: 27-May-93
