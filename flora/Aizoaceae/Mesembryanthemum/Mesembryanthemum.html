Genus: Mesembryanthemum

Genus description:

Herbs. Leaves fleshy, flat or subterete, alternate or oppositer. Flowers solitary or im cymes, Petals numerous. Stigma 5. Ovary inferior; capsule with 5 valves, winged on the angles.
