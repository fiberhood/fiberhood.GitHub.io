Species: Acanthus mollis 

Family: Acanthaceae  
Genus:  Acanthus
Species description: Stems 25-100(-200) cm. Leaves glabrous to puberulent; Basal leaves with lamina 20-60 x 5-15 cm, ovate, pinnatifid, the lobes not narrowed at the base, incise-dentate, long petiolate; upper cauline leaves 1-3 cm, more or less ovate, spinose-dentate, more or less sessile. Bracts c. 4 cm, ovate, usually glabrous. Calyx 4-5 cm, glabrous. Corolla 3.5-5 cm, whitish with purple veins.

Field Notes:

Habitat: Shady places and roadsides.

Distribution: W. & C. Mediterranean region, Portugal. Co Ga Hs It Lu Sa Si [Az Bl]

Found: C.Jugoslavia: fortres near Niksic/ S.Spain: N340 -> Gualiaro

Date: 16-6-76/ 31-5-93
